## Summary

- Adds `treadmill onboarding update <repo>` subcommand with `--worker-deps-python`, `--worker-deps-node`, `--binary name=URL=SHA256@TARGET` (repeatable, additive, deduplicated), and `--clear-worker-deps` (mutually exclusive with dep flags)
- Adds `ApiClient.get_repo_config` (GET `/api/v1/onboarding/repos/{repo}`) and `ApiClient.upsert_repo_config` (POST `/api/v1/onboarding/repos`) to `api_client.py`
- Adds `cli/tests/test_onboarding_update.py` covering binary spec parsing, merge/dedup logic, mutual exclusivity, 404 and 422 error surfaces, and config-field preservation

## Why

ADR-0059 Step 5 specifies the operator CLI surface for managing per-repo worker dependency specs. The `update` subcommand fetches the current `RepoConfig`, merges new specs additively (or clears all when `--clear-worker-deps` is given), and POSTs the result back — completing the round-trip through the onboarding API established in Steps 1-2.
